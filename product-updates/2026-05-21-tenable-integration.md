Introduced a new integration between the **Conviso Platform** and **Tenable**, bringing external scan results into the platform's vulnerability model.

- Ingests Tenable scan data so findings are managed alongside other sources in the platform.
- Particularly valuable for environments that require **auditing** and **PCI** (e.g. ASV scans).
- Documentation is available directly from the platform.
