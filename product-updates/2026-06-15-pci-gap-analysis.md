Launched the **PCI GAP Analysis** feature as part of the **Offensive & Audit Manager** product, enabling teams to run and document the full set of PCI assessments natively in the platform.

- Supports assessments across **PCI DSS, PIN and SSF**.
- Covers **Gap Analysis, SAQ** and the evaluation of the secure software development process — all performed and documented inside the Conviso Platform.
- Each control includes a clickable **Guidance** area with filling instructions, reducing the need for external consulting during self-assessments.
- Keeps the customer working inside the platform instead of relying on exported reports, reinforcing the product's differentiated experience.
