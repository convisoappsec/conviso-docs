## Key benefits

* **Custom Project Requirements:** Tailor security activities directly within each project, allowing for technical adjustments without affecting global organization templates.
* **Enhanced Data Classification:** A major upgrade to our Asset risk engine that incorporates data sensitivity (from Public to Critical) into the final risk score.

---

## Introduction

Release 4.22 focuses on providing the flexibility and precision required for modern AppSec programs. With the launch of **Custom Project Requirements**, we are empowering teams to move away from rigid, "one-size-fits-all" checklists toward a more contextualized security backlog. 

Additionally, we have refined our risk intelligence with **Enhanced Data Classification**. By integrating data sensitivity into the Asset Management workflow, the Conviso Platform now provides a more accurate view of your true risk exposure.

---

## Custom Project Requirements: Security with Context

Standardized security templates are essential for governance, but every project has unique technical nuances. **Custom Project Requirements** allows teams to bridge this gap.

When a requirement template is applied to a project, users can now:
* **Customize Activities:** Edit the descriptions and specific steps of an activity to match the project's tech stack.
* **Refine the Scope:** Ensure that stakeholders only see activities that are relevant to their specific project.
* **Preserve Templates:** All changes are local to the project, meaning your organizational "Golden Templates" remain consistent and secure.

---

## Enhanced Data Classification & Risk Scoring

We have upgraded the risk calculation within our Asset Management module. The risk of a vulnerability is now intrinsically linked to the sensitivity of the data the asset handles.

By using the new **Data Classification** tiers—**Public, Internal, Restricted, Confidential and Critical**—the platform automatically adjusts the Asset's risk priority. This ensures that security teams are always alerted first to threats affecting their most sensitive data environments.
