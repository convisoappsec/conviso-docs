## Introduction

Conviso Platform offers **AI Capabilities** powered by **orchestrated AI agent sets** to support vulnerability analysis, validation, and remediation workflows.

## Where is the AppSec AI Capabilities?

The **AI Capabilities** are available in Conviso Platform through orchestrated AI-agent experiences focused on different security operations.

Instead of relying on a single tool behavior, the platform uses **orchestrated AI agent sets** to:

- support automated vulnerability analysis with business/security context;
- assist false-positive analysis and policy-oriented validation flows;
- accelerate remediation guidance and fix-oriented actions;
- support proactive workflows such as threat modeling.

Check out and try the available AI capabilities in your security workflow.

## Usage
The **AI Capabilities** are available at the top of the Conviso Platform interface, identified by the highlighted icon next to the search field:

<div style={{textAlign: 'center'}}>

![img](../../static/img/platform/appsec-ai-agent-01.png "AI Capabilities")

</div>

1. Log in to Conviso Platform;
2. Locate the **"AppSec AI Agent"** button in the upper header;
3. Click the button to open the AI assistant.

![img](../../static/img/platform/appsec-ai-agent-02.png "AI Capabilities")

The AI assistant will open on the right side of the browser, allowing interaction with Conviso Platform. For better visualization, you can expand the panel by clicking the button with two arrows located in the upper right corner.
To hide the AI assistant, simply click the **"AppSec AI Agent"** button again.
