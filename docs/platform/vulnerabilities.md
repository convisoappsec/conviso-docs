## Introduction

The **Vulnerabilities** area is the main workspace for reviewing security issues identified across applications, infrastructure, and software supply chain components.

From this page, teams can:

* review open vulnerabilities;
* investigate occurrences and evidence;
* track remediation progress;
* update statuses when needed.

## Accessing Vulnerabilities

To access vulnerabilities:

1. In the left navigation menu, click **Vulnerabilities**.

<div style={{textAlign: 'center'}}>

![img](../../static/img/platform/vulnerabilities-img1.png "Vulnerabilities.")

</div>

2. The platform will display the vulnerability list for your company.

:::note
By default, the view lists open vulnerabilities, including statuses such as **Created**, **Identified**, **In Progress**, and **Awaiting Validation**.
:::

## What You Can Do in This View

In the vulnerability list, you can:

* apply quick and advanced filters;
* open grouped occurrences for more detail;
* review technical evidence, timeline, and metadata;
* update vulnerability statuses;
* follow remediation and validation progress.
