## Introduction

Dark Mode is a visual preference feature in the Conviso Platform that allows you to switch the interface to a dark-themed color scheme. This feature is designed to reduce eye strain in low-light environments and provide a personalized visual experience across the entire dashboard and navigation elements.

## Objective

By following this guide, you will be able to enable or disable Dark Mode on the Conviso Platform interface.

## Prerequisites

To manage your theme preferences, you must be logged into the [Conviso Platform](http://conviso.docker.localhost/spa/security-feed).

## Steps

1. Locate the **gear icon (Settings)** in the top navigation bar, situated in the top right section next to the notifications and profile icons.

2. Click the icon to open the settings dropdown menu.

3. Locate the **Dark mode** option at the bottom of the list.

![img](/img/platform/dark-mode-01.png "Step 2: Settings menu with the Dark mode toggle visible.")

4. Use the **toggle switch** next to the "Dark mode" text to enable or disable the feature.

## Validation

After toggling the switch, you should observe the following:

- The platform's theme immediately switches to a dark grey and blue color scheme (if enabled) or reverts to a standard light theme (if disabled).
- The setting is persistent across different pages of the platform and remains active after a page refresh.

![img](/img/platform/dark-mode-02.png "Validation: Conviso Platform interface with Dark Mode enabled.")

## Troubleshooting

If the Dark Mode theme does not apply immediately after toggling the switch, try refreshing your browser page.
